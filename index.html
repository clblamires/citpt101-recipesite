Casey's Cookbook

Welcome to my cookbook! This site has all of my favorite recipes in easy-to-follow steps. I love cookies cooking, and it has become one of my favorite past-times!

I began cooking when I was hungry and there was no one around to feed me. At first, I thought I could just eat a bag of doritos and be okay. After all, doritos are orange and I didn't want to get scurvy. I ate the entire bag of doritos, and felt sick immediately. It wasn't scurvy, obviously, because I was eating something orange (is that how that works?), but I think eating a whole bag of doritos may have been bad for me somehow.

The next time I was hungry, I decided to try cooking something. I had a microwave and a pack of instant ramen. It was... gross. Next time I was hungry, I wanted to redo the ramen and see what I could do to improve it. I discovered that the stove is not as scary as I once originally thought. Here's a tip for any new chefs out there: if you're afraid of getting burned by the stove top, don't put your hand on it! Once I figured that out, a whole new world opened up to me!

Now, I have all sorts of really cool appliances in my kitchen that aren't scary at all!

  • Blender
  • Bigger microwave
  • Mixer
  • Air fryer
  • Pasta maker that I don't actually know how to use
  • Electric tea kettle
  • And more!

Follow my recipes and I guarantee that you might or might not make decent food!
